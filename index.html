Welcome to my Monster Hunter shrine!

This is my fanmade quickstart guide to newcomers of the Monster Hunter franchise! Monster Hunter is an intimidating series to get into, with roughly 20 years of world building and game mechanics. Digesting this and 20 years worth of fan in-jokes and terminology is enough to scare some newcomers off.
